About Me

Passionate About Story Telling with Data

I consider myself lucky to be born and raised in Southern California, to be brought up in a place primed with cultural diversity. Even so, there was a unique insanity to growing up with a younger sister with special needs and parents that spoke little to no English, an experience that not only reflected poorly on my grades but also shook my confidence in academia altogether. Because of this, I came to understand that grades did not tell the whole story about me as a person and that no single datapoint could capture lessons learned from such experiences. Instead, my situation taught me something more valuableâ€”tenacity and resilience, which I carried with me as I traversed different fields of study and professional environments.
In learning to find my own voice in advocating for myself and my family, I also cultivated a skill for communication. From creative writing, to bioinformatics, and to software/financial technology, I found the capacity to grow and adapt in each setting despite the challenges presented. In doing so, I recognized that my professional brand has developed over the years from clumsy communicator to storytelling with data, translating something highly technical for the everyday user.
